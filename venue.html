Directions

By Plane

The Kittilä airport is a 15-minute drive away from Levi's mountain village. An airport shuttle bus runs from the Kittilä airport to Levi after all scheduled flights (see Tunturilinjat). Taxis and car rentals are available at the airport. A taxi from Kittilä airport to Levi center costs about 50-70 EUR.

You can also reach Levi from Rovaniemi airport by bus (170 km from Levi; see Eskelisen Lapin linjat).

By Train

The closest train station is in Kolari, from where you can get to Levi by bus (80 km; see Tunturilinjat). Kolari is served by night trains from Helsinki. Alternatively, you can use the Rovaniemi station (170 km from Levi).

Local Transport & Logistics

  • Tickets are sold on the airport shuttle buses and online.
  • If you take the shuttle bus from Kittilä airport to Levi, the closest bus stop to the venue is called "Leviraitti".
  • Taxis and car rentals are available at the airport.
